WHO AM I?

How I came to be a Designer and Developer

  • Earlier...

    I worked in Travel

    Starting out at Flight Centre Limited arranging travel plans for the leisure market, I eventually found myself the dedicated Corporate Travel Consultant for KMPG Australia and DuPont at HRG Worldwide.

  • Next...

    I became a designer

    It was at the Design Centre Enmore that I developed my design and Illustration skills. Around the same time, I set up Imagined By Andrew, my freelance design business which I continue to own and operate.

  • Finally...

    I am a Developer

    Having completed General Assembly's Web Development Immersive, I now have both the back-end and the front-end skills to design and build complex applications for web.

  • Today

    With my skills combined...

    I'm in the unique position of being a qualified Graphic Designer and Illustrator who has professional experience to work alongside any team and the development skills to translate creative concepts all the way from print to web.

BINGER

The premiere app for TV lovers

The Binger mission is to provide tv fans with a way of keeping track of season premiere dates by way of calendar-synchronisation and push-notifications.
